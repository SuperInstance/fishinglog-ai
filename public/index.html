Pacific Northwest & Alaska

Built for Alaskan Waters

FishingLog.ai is purpose-built for the fisheries of the Pacific Northwest and Alaska. From seasonal salmon trolling in Southeast Alaska to longline operations in the Gulf, every model, alert, and report is tuned for the species and regulations you actually work with.

Supports salmon troll, longline, and trawl operations with species libraries and reporting templates specific to each gear type. ADFG fish tickets, NOAA logbooks, and IFQ tracking -- all generated from your actual catch data.
